About the company

A family company with
tradition and vision

Marco doo is a family company from Novi Sad, founded in 1993. From the very beginning, our mission was clear — to provide quality equipment for the Serbian market, with a professional approach to every client and project.

Representing renowned global gas equipment manufacturers, Marco doo provides complete solutions — from consulting and design to delivery and technical support.
